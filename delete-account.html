Account & Data Deletion — Clothing Catalyst

Clothing Catalyst — Catalyse Your Style (by Unreal Vision Studio). This page explains how to delete your account and what data will be removed.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the Clothing Catalyst mobile app.
  2. Go to Profile → Delete Account.
  3. Follow the on-screen confirmation steps. Deleting your account is permanent.

If you cannot access the app, you may request deletion by emailing info@unrealvisionstudio.com. Please include the email address associated with your account and the phrase "Account Deletion Request" in the subject line.

Data that will be deleted

When you delete your account via the app, we will permanently delete the following data from our active systems and cloud storage:

  • Account profile (email, display name, settings)
  • Uploaded wardrobe photos and images stored on Amazon S3
  • Clothing items, tags (type, material, style, weight) and recommendations
  • User-generated preferences and app-specific metadata

Data that may be retained

In certain limited cases we may retain some information for legal, security or business reasons:

  • Transaction or billing records (if applicable) — retained only as required by law.
  • Aggregate, anonymized analytics that cannot be tied back to an individual user.
  • Short-term logs or backups: we remove data from active storage immediately and purge associated backups within 30 days unless retention is required by law.

How we delete S3 data

When an account is deleted we remove the user's images from our Amazon S3 buckets and delete related database records. We also run a background job to ensure eventual consistency and remove any copies from caches. This process typically completes within 24–72 hours.

If you see any content that you believe should have been deleted after that window, contact info@unrealvisionstudio.com and we will investigate promptly.

Questions or support

If you need help deleting your account or have questions about data retention, email info@unrealvisionstudio.com with the subject Account Deletion Request. We aim to respond within 2 business days.
